Vivinithia

Vivinithia, is my first attempt at game development. The game is a classic turn based RPG in a fantasy setting. The story and world are orignial creations. The assets are borrowed since I was just trying to make a proof of concept. The music is from one of my favorite RPGs; Golden Sun. The art assets are from the engine I build the game in, RPG Maker MV
